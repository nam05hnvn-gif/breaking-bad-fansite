About Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is an American crime drama television series created by Vince Gilligan. It follows Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine producer, and his partner Jesse Pinkman as they navigate the dangers of the criminal underworld.

Main Characters


Walter White

The protagonist, also known as Heisenberg.


Jesse Pinkman

Skyler White

Walter's wife.


Hank Schrader

DEA agent and Walter's brother-in-law.
